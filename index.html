About our founder

Beau's Bon bons candy store originated from a french-born factory worker named Beau Bateau. He invented them during the times of the gummy bear wars that were prominent in Sweden. He revolutionized the idea of sugar candy and the swedes were especially captivated with all of his delicious inventions.
